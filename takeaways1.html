Guha et al. 2016

Schema.org is fast becoming a key instrument in the web application branch. Evidence suggests that schema.org is among the most important factors for data structuring on the web (GUHA, R.V, et al. 2016). History has shown how difficult it was to find relevant data through an information platform on the web (GUHA, R.V, et al. 2016). This paper compares the different between the past and the recent demand of schema.org since the increased interest in big data. Nevertheless, schema.org shows also several development the past few years giving more opportunities and makes it more popular in usage (GUHA, R.V, et al. 2016). This gives me more the impression that since the increased interest in big data, schema.org re-developed itself and keeps up with the time (GUHA, R.V, et al. 2016). The supporting teams such as google and Microsoft helping schema.org played a necessary role within its development (GUHA, R.V, et al. 2016).
